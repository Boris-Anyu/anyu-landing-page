Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Refund Policy explains how ANYU may review payment issues, incorrect charges, failed trips, duplicate payments, and eligible refund requests.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered for duplicate charges, failed payment errors, cancelled trips charged incorrectly, serious service failures, or confirmed overcharges.

Non-Refundable Situations

Completed rides are generally not refundable unless there is a confirmed payment, safety, or service issue. Promotions and credits may have separate conditions.

Cash Payments

For cash rides, ANYU may investigate disputes but may not be able to reverse cash already exchanged between rider and driver. Account credits may be issued at ANYU’s discretion.

Wallet and Digital Payments

Eligible refunds may be returned to the original payment method, ANYU Wallet, or ride credits depending on the payment method and local availability.

How to Request a Refund

Contact ANYU through the app, website, WhatsApp, or admin@getanyu.com with trip details, payment method, and reason for the request.

Review Time

ANYU will review requests as quickly as possible. Processing times may vary depending on payment providers, banks, mobile money operators, and investigation needs.
