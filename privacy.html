ANYU (“ANYU,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our website, mobile applications, platform, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, phone number, email address, profile photo, and account details.
  • Location information used for pickup, drop-off, matching, trip tracking, safety, and support.
  • Trip details, including pickup location, destination, route, distance, duration, fare, and driver/rider details.
  • Payment-related information, including payment method, transaction status, wallet activity, refunds, credits, and promotions.
  • Device information, app activity, browser type, IP address, crash logs, and technical diagnostics.
  • Communications with ANYU, including support messages, feedback forms, WhatsApp messages, reports, and complaints.
  • Driver information, where applicable, including identity documents, vehicle information, license details, insurance documents, and verification records.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Match riders with drivers.
  • Provide ride booking, scheduled rides, trip tracking, and ride history.
  • Process payments, refunds, wallet transactions, promotions, and referral credits.
  • Support safety features, including SOS, emergency contacts, trip sharing, ride PIN, and incident reporting.
  • Verify drivers, vehicles, and platform activity.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, unsafe behavior, and unauthorized activity.
  • Provide customer support and respond to questions or complaints.
  • Improve ANYU’s products, pricing, operations, safety, and user experience.
  • Send service updates, launch updates, promotional messages, and important account notices where permitted.

3. Location Data

ANYU may use location data before, during, and after trips to support accurate pickups, driver matching, live tracking, trip sharing, route visibility, emergency tools, fraud prevention, and customer support.

4. Safety and Emergency Features

To support rider and driver safety, ANYU may process information related to emergency contacts, shared trip links, live location, ride PIN usage, incident reports, driver verification, vehicle information, and support investigations.

5. How We Share Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Riders and drivers involved in a trip.
  • Payment processors, mobile money providers, banks, and wallet service providers.
  • Identity verification, fraud prevention, analytics, hosting, communication, and support providers.
  • Emergency contacts selected by the user when trip sharing or emergency tools are used.
  • Law enforcement, regulators, courts, or government authorities when legally required or necessary for safety.
  • Business partners only where needed to provide or improve ANYU services.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Payments and Financial Information

ANYU may support payment methods such as cash, mobile money, wallet credits, cards, or other local payment options. Payment processing may be handled by third-party providers. ANYU does not store full card numbers unless legally permitted and technically required through secure providers.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, disclosure, or alteration. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, investigate safety incidents, prevent fraud, maintain business records, and enforce agreements.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information. Some requests may be limited by legal, safety, fraud prevention, or operational requirements.

10. Marketing Communications

If you join our waitlist, submit feedback, or request early access, we may contact you about ANYU updates, launch information, promotions, or related services. You may opt out where required or available.

11. Children’s Privacy

ANYU is not intended for children under the legal age required to use transportation services independently. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

12. International Expansion

ANYU is being built with a global vision. As we expand to additional countries, data practices may be updated to comply with local laws and market requirements.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on our website or app with a revised “Last updated” date.
